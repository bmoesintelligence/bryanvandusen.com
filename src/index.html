~ about the author ~

A storyteller raised among forests, animals, and a great deal of imagination.

Bryan grew up in a corner of the world where the woods did most of the talking — long afternoons under tall trees, conversations with creatures who didn't seem to mind the company, and a slow, steady habit of paying attention.

Now he writes picture books for children who are figuring out who they are, and for the grown-ups who love them. His stories are soft, sturdy places where being different is the whole point, and every reader is invited to feel completely, unmistakably seen.

a book, he hopes, can be a
